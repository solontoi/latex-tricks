
% ### I sometimes want to write roman numerals without it
% ### looking like the capital I and V of the current font.

\makeatletter
\newcommand*{\rom}[1]{\expandafter\@slowromancap\romannumeral #1@}
\makeatother

% ### Usage

The Roman Numeral for ``1'' \rom{1}.
The Roman Numeral for ``27'' \rom{27}.
